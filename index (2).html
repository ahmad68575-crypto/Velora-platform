Our Story

Built for Real People

Velora started as a small experiment in 2023 — a question: what would social media look like if it actually put you first?

01

The Mission

We believe social media should energize, not drain. Velora is built around connection over consumption — every feature is designed to help you share meaningfully, not mindlessly scroll.

02

The Team

A crew of 40 designers, engineers, and community builders across 12 countries. We are independent, venture-backed, and obsessed with making something people genuinely love.

03

The Vision

A world where every voice has a home. We are building toward a platform where culture is created by everyone — not just the loudest or most followed accounts.
